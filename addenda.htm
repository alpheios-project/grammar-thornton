ADDENDA ET CORRIGENDA.

§ 27. لَهُ الْمُلْكْ وَلَهُ الْحمْدْ The reference is to Corân, Sûraħ lxiv. verse 1.

§ 28. يُسَبِّحُ The reference is to Corân, Sûraħ lxic. verse 1.

§ 43, Rem. c. جَاوَزْنَا بِ The reference is to Corân, Sûraħ vii. verse 134.

§ 45. Dele سَلِمَ to be safe, أَسْلَمَ to preserve.

§ 45. REM. c. For أَسْلَمَ to turn Moslem from إِسْلَامٌ Islâm read أَسْلَمَ to obtain peace, to surrender one's self (to God) from سِلْمٌ peace.

§ 444, REM. g. For Koran read Corân.

§ 456. For أَجِيهِ (p. 169, 1.11) read أَخِيهِ

§ 470. Add, REM. f. مَا is often inserted after عَنْ مِنْ and بِ without affecting their regimen and is then called مَا الزَّائِدَةُ the superfluous mâ (see § 353*).
